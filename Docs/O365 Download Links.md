**Direct downloads to the online installers for Office 365 with no login requirements.**

End user licenses will adjust most features regardless which version gets installed, making [O365 Business (x64)](https://c2rsetup.officeapps.live.com/c2r/download.aspx?ProductreleaseID=O365BusinessRetail&platform=x64&language=en-us&version=O16GA) a fairly universal installer for 99% of business clients. The following short URL will also help downloading that version directly onto client devices.

https://xga.io/365
